Convenient to Downtown Orlando, we are located at the corner of Colonial Drive and Altaloma Avenue.

Our address is:1801 E. Colonial Drive, Orlando, Florida 32803

From I-4: take Exit #83B to Colonial Drive(Hwy 50). Go East on Colonial two miles to the intersection with Mills (17-92). Continue two blocks then turn left on Altaloma Avenue.

From Orlando International Airport: Take South Semoran (436) North for eight miles to Colonial Drive (Hwy 50). Turn left(West) and proceed three miles to Altaloma Ave.
